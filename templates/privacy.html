Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly to track expenses, we store the data you enter: amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you add.

We do not collect payment information, and we do not track your activity outside of the Spendly application.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking features of Spendly — displaying your records, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your finances. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

If we ever introduce third-party integrations in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be informed before any data is shared.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
